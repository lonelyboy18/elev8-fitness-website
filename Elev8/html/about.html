Our Story

About ELEV8

Goa's premium calisthenics studio, built on movement quality, discipline and community. We started on park benches and grew into a structured training home for athletes at every level.

How It Started

From park benches
to a training movement

ELEV8 was born when a small group of friends in Goa discovered the transformative power of bar work and street workouts. What began as informal sessions in open spaces evolved into a commitment to structured, progressive calisthenics training.

Today, our coaches guide newcomers and seasoned athletes through programs that build real confidence and skill — one deliberate rep at a time. We believe in the power of your own body.

ELEV8 Calisthenics — the movement in motion.
